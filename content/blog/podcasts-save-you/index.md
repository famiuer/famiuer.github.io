In my second year of college, I got an iPod Shuffle and discovered podcasts through iTunes.
That first exposure did not impress me, but later I found many high-quality shows and built
a long-term habit around them.

A few podcasts I recommended at the time:

- [The Civil Engineering Podcast](https://engineeringmanagementinstitute.org/cep-podcast/)
- [SINICA](https://supchina.com/series/sinica/)
- [MERICS](https://www.merics.org/)
- [China Tech Talk](https://chinatechtalk.libsyn.com/)

There are many great podcasts. The key is finding voices that consistently teach you something new.
